Gattopardonero

We provide the full suite of competitive logistics, financing and risk management services while sharing upside with our clients.

Gattopardonero, a 100% subsidiary of Gattopardonero, was established in 2020 and is headquartered in Hong Kong - the center of the international energy commodity trading community. Gattopardonero aspires to become one of the world’s most competitive, reliable and admired providers of energy, logistics, financing and risk management services.

Our Business

Gattopardonero trades all parts of the barrel from crude oil to light-ends, middle distillates and heavy ends as well as LNG and dry-bulk energy commodities. We have a deep understanding of the molecules we trade and excel at logistics and blending. Our multitude of skills in trade finance and risk management help create value for our suppliers and clients.
